My Statement

I believe creativity can enhance the mind. Unfortunately some schools cannot afford art programs so I like to bring lessons to students that do not have access to art. I have taught classes in elementary schools and at a homeless shelter. Art is therapeutic, and for some kids it is their only outlet. My aim is to awaken a creative way of thinking and problem solving which students can use even outside of the art world. Often times I work with recycled or donated materials. Besides teaching, I am a also a student at SJSU majoring in art. My greatest work is helping someone else feel a sense of accomplishment.
